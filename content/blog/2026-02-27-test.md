Join us for a hands-on training session to introduce OrangeBoy 
Savannah. Due to overwhelming interest in this training, we are limiting
 participation to 5 attendees per library. To identify participants, 
please see our guide: [Should I attend OrangeBoy Savannah Training?](https://www.ccslib.org/sites/default/files/2026-01/Should%20I%20attend%20OrangeBoy%20Savannah%20Training.pdf) \*\*

This session will include: 

* Review: What is Savannah, and why are we/how will we/who will be using it?
* How to access and navigate OrangeBoy Savannah.
* What are Clusters?
* Information about standard reports and how to use them.
* Standard reports currently include summary data for ILS and econtent use*
* Market Watch Reports: Mapping active cardholders and census data
